About Us

History

Sea Mist Haven was founded with a vision of blending luxury with the serene beauty of nature, inspired by the unspoiled coastline. The hotel's design integrates local culture, sustainability, and traditional architecture, honoring both the environment and local heritage. From the beginning, there’s been a commitment to eco-friendly practices and deep ties to the local community, supporting local artisans, farmers, and fishermen. What began as a simple dream evolved into a stunning tropical retreat, marked by heartfelt hospitality and a dedication to creating memorable experiences. Each milestone reflects a journey rooted in passion, patience, and a love for the region's natural beauty.

Location

Nestled along a breathtaking stretch of coastline, Sea Mist Haven boasts unparalleled access to both lush tropical forests and tranquil sandy beaches. With a backdrop of vibrant sunsets and crystal-clear waters, our hotel is perfectly positioned for exploring the wonders of the natural world, whether you're snorkeling in coral reefs or hiking through scenic trails.
